
Dependencies:
    SDL2 (SDL2_Image, SDL2_Mixer)


Controls:
	WASD:         Move forwards, left, back, and right
	Left Key:     Rotate camera left
	Right Key:    Rotate camera right
    Mouse:        Rotate camera
    Space:        Action (e.g. open doors)
    ALt-Enter:    Fullscreen
    P:            Pause
    Alt-F4 or Esc:Closes the game
